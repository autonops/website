Frequently asked questions

What's included in the free trial?

You get full access to Pro features for 30 days. No credit card required. After the trial, you can continue with the free tier or upgrade.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. When upgrading, you'll be prorated. When downgrading, changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.

Is my data secure?

InfraIQ runs locally on your machine. Your credentials, infrastructure data, and configurations never leave your environment. See our security page for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards. For Enterprise annual contracts, we also accept wire transfers and invoicing.

Do you offer startup discounts?

Yes! Early-stage startups may qualify for extended trials or discounted rates. Contact us to learn more.
